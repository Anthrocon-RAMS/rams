Supporter Badges

The Supporter Badge is a way for dedicated fans of MAGFest to give us a little extra cash to help make the convention better. In exchange, we try to reward you with some swag and special keepsakes.

We don't announce everything that Supporters get in advance, but we always give away most of the swag available at our store, such as a tshirt and sticker, and we always make sure to give a few special items not available to non-Supporters.

We also let you identify an affiliate; basically, this tells us which groups are throwing the most financial support into MAGFest so that we can reciprocate by doing cool things for those groups.
