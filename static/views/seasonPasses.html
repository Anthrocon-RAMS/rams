2014 Supporter Season Passes

This year we're doing something new; when you decide to kick in extra, there's a donation level where we throw in a Season Pass for all events run by MAGFest. This includes MAGStock, Bitgen, and Game Over.

You'll be emailed before each event and given a chance to fill out a form to claim your complementary tickets. These tickets are non-transferable; you're welcome to claim them for yourself but may not sell or transfer them to anyone else.
