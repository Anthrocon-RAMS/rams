What You Get For Volunteering

The more hours you work, the more you get (don't forget about weighted hours):
  • Working at least 6 hours gets you a set of MAGFest swag: a sticker and tshirt.

  • Working at least 12 hours gets you food in addition to the swag. We have professional chefs preparing 3 meals per day throughout the con, although our current venue severly limits our options, and for the most part we are unable to serve hot food.

  • Working at least 18 hours gets you a refund on your badge (whatever the price of a regular attendee badge was when you registered) instead of the swag. You still get the food, of course.

  • Working at least 24 hours gets you everything; the badge refund, tshirt, food, and swag.
