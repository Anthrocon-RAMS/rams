Fire Safety Certification

The state of Maryland requires all Magfest staff to complete an online training course from the Maryland fire marshal on crowd/fire safety. There's no test; you just read through some slides. At the end, you are presented with a certificate, which contains your certification number.

Complete the course at http://firemarshal.state.md.us/crowdmanager/ and then enter your certification number below. {% popup_link ../static/fire_cert_example.png "Click here" %} to see an example certificate so you'll know where to look for the certification number.
